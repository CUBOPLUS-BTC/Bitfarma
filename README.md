💊 ¿Qué es BitFarma?
BitFarma es una aplicación web para la gestión integral de una farmacia moderna, 
donde los usuarios pueden cargar sus recetas o prescripciones médicas de forma digital para que el farmacéutico las prepare,
y gestione la entrega, ya sea retiro en tienda o envío a domicilio.
Lo que hace única a BitFarma es su integración con Bitcoin como medio de pago, 
combinando la soberanía financiera con la soberanía sobre tu salud.
"Porque tu salud y tu dinero te pertenecen."

COMMON MONETIZATION MODELS FOR AN APPLICATION
In-App Advertising: Developers display ads (banners, pop-ups, or rewarded videos) and earn money based on user engagement, such as views or clicks. This method generates the majority of global app revenue.
Freemium & Subscriptions: The app is free to download, but users pay a recurring subscription or one-time fee to access premium features, remove ads, or unlock content.
In-App Purchases (IAP): Users purchase virtual items, currency, or special features within the app (e.g., lives in a game or special filters in a camera app).
Paid Apps: A small percentage of apps require a one-time purchase to download.
Data Licensing & Affiliate Marketing: Some apps make money by analyzing anonymized user data or by referring users to other services. Reddit +4
How They Maximize Earnings
App Store Optimization (ASO): Developers optimize their app listing with keywords to improve visibility, as seen in successful apps like Habit Kit, which uses this to drive organic downloads.
Building in Public: Sharing development updates on social media (like TikTok or Instagram) is a common strategy to build a user base before launch.
Leveraging AI: Many developers are now creating AI-powered tools that offer high value, allowing for higher subscription costs.
Platforms & Tools: Developers often use platforms like Flutter for cross-platform development (iOS and Android) to maximize reach, and platforms like Google Ads for monetization. YouTube +4
The most successful applications often use a hybrid model, offering a free, ad-supported experience while nudging users toward paid subscriptions to remove ads or access enhanced functionality. YouTube
